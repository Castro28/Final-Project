Chamoy Rim Dip

Our pineapple and strawberry-flavored chamoy rim dips are both made with real fruits, creating a delicious and authentic taste that's sure to satisfy your sweet and spicy cravings. For the pineapple chamoy rim dip, we blend fresh, ripe pineapples with our homemade chamoy sauce, which features a unique blend of chili peppers, lime juice, and spices. The result is a tropical and tangy dip that's perfect for rimming your favorite drinks. And for the strawberry chamoy rim dip, we puree plump, ripe strawberries and blend them with our homemade chamoy sauce to create a sweet and spicy dip that's perfect for rimming fruity cocktails. Whether you're a fan of pineapple or strawberry, our chamoy rim dips are sure to impress and add a delicious twist to your favorite drinks. Try them today and experience the mouthwatering taste of real fruits combined with the unique flavor of chamoy!

Agua Fresca

Our all-natural cucumber drink is a refreshing and healthy choice that's perfect for those hot summer days. And for an extra kick of flavor, we dip the rim of the cup in our homemade chamoy sauce, which features a blend of chili peppers, lime juice, and spices. The result is a delicious and unique combination of sweet and spicy flavors that's sure to wake up your taste buds. So whether you're looking for a healthy and refreshing drink, or just want to try something new and delicious, our cucumber drink with chamoy rim dip is the perfect choice!
